Beginners' Latin: Activities

Have fun with Latin!

Choose an activity and test your skill. Each activity is associated with a lesson in the tutorial. If you prefer to read the lesson first, click through on the link provided.

No previous knowledge of Latin is required - this tutorial is suitable for anyone who wants to learn.
