Beginners' Latin: Lesson 1

Lesson 1: Verbs

These lessons cover the type of Latin used in official documents written in England between 1086 and 1733. We recommend that you begin with lesson 1 and work through to lesson 12. Lesson 1 is the most simple; lesson 12 is the most difficult.

No previous knowledge of Latin is required - this tutorial is suitable for anyone who wants to learn.
